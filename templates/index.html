Introduction

What is it
It's a Football Tournament. Hosted by students, our Aim is to create a fun, and enjoyable experience for all. Gear up for hours of intense play, with 5-a-Side matches in a division setting to ensure everyone gets to play to their hearts' content. May the odds be ever in your favor.
Sports
Your team can consist of upto 9 players, with 5-a-side teams played on a turf. Each match will be about 20 minutes long, and the more you win, the more you play. Like division football (i.e like the UEFA Champions' League), there will be a group category, then Quarterfinals, Semifinals and Finals.
Prizes
Exciting cash prizes and vouchers for the Winners and Runners ups!

FAQ

  • Registration questions
  • Event questions
  • Miscellaneous questions
  • What is the registration fee for the tournaments?
    The registration fee for the football event is ₹2899 per team. The registration fee for the chess tournament depends whether you have an UKCA ID or not. The initial fee is ₹2899 but you have to pay ₹50 extra if you don't have the ID. The registration fee for the chess tournament is yet to be decided.
  • How do I pay the amount?
    Before making your payment, you need to have registered on our site. Once that's done, there are two ways to make the payment - one is by PayTM and the other is by cash. If you chose the appropriate payment method during the registration, instructions on how to make the transfer should have been sent to the registered email account with all the other necessary details.
  • If I've made the payment through PayTM, how do I know I've done so?
    After you've made the payment through PayTM, one of our representative will update that you've done so and you should receive an email regarding the completion of your payment
  • Where is the tournament happening/What is the venue?
    The venue is the Turf Park in Koramangala.
  • How long will the event last?
    The event will last 2 days, over the weeekend of the 14th and 15th April, 2018. If you win on day 1 and qualify for the next round, you will be required to come on day 2. If you do not qualify on day 1, you may come on day 2 to spectate.
  • Who is the event being organised by?
    The event is being organised by a group of students.
