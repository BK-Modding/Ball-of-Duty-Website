Introduction

What it is
Well, it's a football tournament! Hosted by students, our aim is to create a fun, and enjoyable experience for all! Gear up for hours of footballing fun, with 5-a-side matches in a division setting to ensure everyone gets to play to their hearts' content!May the odds be ever in your favor!
Sports
Football/Soccer! Your team can consist of upto 9 players, with 5-a-side teams played on an awesome turf. Each match will be 20 minutes long, and the more you win, the more you play! Like division football (i.e like the EPL), there will be a group category, then quarterfinals, semifinals and finals!
Prizes
Trophies for the first, second, and third places! For the rest, I don't know as of yet lol.
