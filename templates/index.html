Introduction

What it is
Well, it's a football tournament! Hosted by students, our aim is to create a fun, and enjoyable experience for all! Gear up for hours of footballing fun, with 5-a-side matches in a division setting to ensure everyone gets to play to their hearts' content!May the odds be ever in your favor!
Sports
Football Your team can consist of upto 9 players, with 5-a-side teams played on a turf. Each match will be about 20 minutes long, and the more you win, the more you play! Like division football (i.e like the EPL), there will be a group category, then quarterfinals, semifinals and finals!
Prizes
Exciting cash prizes and vouchers for the winners and runners ups!
